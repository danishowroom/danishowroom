丹麦商品中心简介

为整合利用丹麦国丰富先进的商务资源及商品资源，发挥上海在中国特别是长三角地区巨大的商业资源及平台效应，响应上海市政府打造上海国际贸易中心的动议，在中丹两国政府支持下，丹麦商品中心于2011年11月18日正式开张。 来自丹麦的健康、安全、有机、营养的高品质食品将从此走进中国消费者的生活。

丹麦商品中心的主要功能为以丹麦商品为主的展览展示交易，不定期举办专题文化经贸论坛，促进文化交流。 除产品展示、会务服务及商务服务外，还将在此安排各项与中国各大经销商、买家及零售商的商务接洽活动。高品质食品与饮料市场在中国发展很快，本中心也将为丹麦品牌进入中国市场提供巨大的商机。

丹麦商品中心是丹麦政府对外支持设立的第一家国别商品中心，致力于推进两国间优秀商品，技术，运营模式之间的相互交流及业务拓展，使其成为两国间的一个窗口及平台。

Introducing Danish Showroom

In order to fully utilize Denmark’s abundant and advanced business and commodity resources, to seize Shanghai’s huge commercial resources and platform effect in China and especially in the Yangtze River delta, to respond to Shanghai’s Government’s motion of making Shanghai an international trade center, Danish Showroom was opened on Nov 18, 2011, under the support of both the Chinese and Danish government. Healthy, safe, organic, nutritious, and premium food from Denmark has since then entered the life of Chinese consumers.

The main purpose of Danish Showroom is to show and trade primarily Danish commodities, to host irregular cultural and trade forums on special subjects, and to promote cultural exchange. Apart from displaying commodities, committee affairs services, and business services, Danish Showroom will also arrange various business consultation events for buyers, retailers, and distributors. The market for high quality food and beverage is growing fast in China, and so Danish Showroom will provide enormous business opportunities for Danish brands entering the Chinese market.

Danish Showroom is the first external nation-level commodity center established and supported by the Danish Government, and is dedicated to promoting the exchange of premium commodities, technologies, and business models, and the exploration of professional work between China and Denmark, so as to act as a window, or even a platform, for both countries.

同时，丹麦商品中心也会将优秀的丹麦及欧洲商品带给中国！让这些著名的品牌从她的诞生地来到中国。
